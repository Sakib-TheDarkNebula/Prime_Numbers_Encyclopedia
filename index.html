WHAT IS A PRIME NUMBER..?

A prime Number refers to the whole numbers greater than 1, which are only divisible by 1 and that number. More simply, Suppose x is a Prime Number. So it must be divisible by only 1 and x. Here are some examples of Prime Numbers:

  • 3 >> you can only divide it by 1 and 3
  • 5 >> you can only divide it by 1 and 5
  • 113 >> You can only divide it by 1 and 113!
